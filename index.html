Scorecard Guide

          
Score each answer 1–5. AI dimensions are weighted 2× for Staff+ roles. For Engineering roles, Dim 2 (Hands-On) is a required dimension at all levels.

          

            

              Strong Yes — Exceptional
              
Pioneers AI use, concrete examples, ships AI-powered work, promotes best practices.

            

            

              Yes — Proficient
              
Consistent AI usage with measurable outcomes, adopts tools quickly, solid hands-on.

            

            

              No — Borderline
              
Occasional use, lacks systematic thinking, limited trend awareness. May affect leveling.

            

            

              Strong No — Not Recommended
              
Rarely uses AI tools, no self-directed learning. Strong No for AI-native org.

            

          

          

          
Governance Principle

          
AI is a MUST at OKX — strong tech + no AI may still pass but will affect leveling. Strong AI cannot compensate for weak fundamentals.

Full Scoring Definitions

          

            

              
5 — Exemplary

              
Actively pioneers AI use. Clear, recent, specific examples. Integrates AI into daily workflow. Has shipped AI-powered work with measurable outcomes.

            

            

              
4 — Proficient

              
Consistent AI tool usage with measurable outcomes. Understands limitations. Adopts new tools quickly and with good judgment.

            

            

              
3 — Developing

              
Some AI exposure. Uses tools occasionally. Curious but not yet systematic. Would benefit from structured onboarding to AI-native workflows.

            

            

              
2 — Limited

              
Aware of AI but passive. No concrete examples in CV or experience. Unlikely to self-initiate AI adoption without strong direction.

            

            

              
1 — None

              
No evidence of AI usage, interest, or mindset. May be actively skeptical. Not a strong fit for an AI-native organisation.

            

          

        

      

    


    
    

      

        

          
KATCH Competency Scorecard

          
OKG-wide competency framework — Knowledge · Acumen · Teamwork · Cognition · Hunger

        

      


      

        What is KATCH? KATCH defines what great looks like at OKG — a centralized competency framework guiding hiring, development, and performance. Each competency is scored 1–5 per interviewer and aggregated into an overall KATCH score used alongside JD match and AI nativeness.
